Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Your Privacy Matters

At FocusFlow, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use our Pomodoro timer application. We are committed to transparency and protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Data

FocusFlow stores your session statistics (focus count, total minutes, current streak) locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is only used to display your progress. We do not have access to this information.

No Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or contact details. You can use FocusFlow completely anonymously without creating an account.

Third-Party Services

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display the Outfit typeface on our website. When you visit FocusFlow, Google may collect technical information such as your IP address as part of providing this service. This is governed by Google's privacy policy.

Advertising Services

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense or similar third-party advertising services. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to display relevant ads based on your browsing activity. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Cookies and Tracking

FocusFlow itself does not use cookies for tracking purposes. However, third-party services such as advertising networks may use cookies to serve personalized ads. These cookies are not under our control.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of third-party features but will not impact the core timer functionality.

Data Security

Since all your session data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage, you have full control over it. We do not store any of your data on our servers. Clearing your browser data will remove all FocusFlow statistics from your device.

Children's Privacy

FocusFlow does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Our service is available to users of all ages, but we do not specifically target children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and delete your locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser storage
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through browser settings or ad network preferences
  • Use FocusFlow without providing any personal information
  • Request information about how third-party services handle your data

International Users

FocusFlow is accessible worldwide. If you are accessing our service from outside the United States, please be aware that any information processed by third-party services may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using FocusFlow, you consent to such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

Email: privacy@focusflow.app

Or visit our Contact Page

Summary:

FocusFlow respects your privacy. We don't collect your personal data. Your statistics stay on your device. Third-party services like ads may use cookies. You have full control over your data.
